## Notes

- **No individual founder portrait.** `dsc01625.webp` is a formal team group; `dsc01551.webp` shows a supervisor but as a briefing scene, not a clean founder headshot. If the V5 About section wants a named-founder photo, this is a gap.
- **No bottled-product shots at all.** Four of the seven V5 product cards (Products 01, 02, 05, 07) lack dedicated photography. For V1 wabi-sabi this is tolerable (hand-drawn/illustrative substitutes work); for V4 premium industrial it is a real problem and the shot-list should prioritize these.
- **No farmland / sourcing landscape.** The "seed sourcing" process step has no true landscape. `dsc00723.webp` (hand with seeds) is the closest and should be reused.
- **DSC01084 is the strongest single photo in the set** — moody amber oil-drip with leading lines, works beautifully for V1 (warm), V3 (archival/editorial) and even V4 (dramatic facility detail). Flag for prominent use across all themes.
- **P1455288 silos are unusually cinematic** — rust patina, low angle, tree framing. Strong hero in all four themes; in V2 B&W the tonal range is excellent.
- **DSC00904** (chaff in motion) is a rare "live process" shot with particulate motion — worth flagging for V3 magazine-editorial especially.
- **DSC01625 (team group)** is a flat overcast group portrait; weakest for V2 B&W (low contrast, flat) but still usable with toning.
- **Heavy on process/interior, very light on exterior and product.** The photo pool skews toward factory-floor storytelling; product and sourcing need supplementation.
