I.
Section 01 · Origin

A specialist house, built
around one crop.

Shufola Multi Products Ltd is a specialist Processor and Exporter of Sesame Seeds, Sesame Oil, and Sesame Oil Cake, headquartered in Dhaka. Our entire operation is purpose-built around sesame — from procurement through processing to international shipment.

Our factory in Bhodrokul, Ullapara, Shirajgonj sits at the heart of one of Bangladesh's most fertile sesame-growing regions. With a port office in Chattogram, we maintain direct control over logistics, documentation, and quality at every stage.

GACC Registered. Halal Certified. Every shipment fully documented. Every buyer gets a focused, expert partner.

Seed Sourcing
