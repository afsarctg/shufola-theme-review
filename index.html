About

A company built
around one thing

Shufola Multi Products Ltd is a specialist sesame oil manufacturer and exporter headquartered in Dhaka. Our entire operation is purpose-built around sesame — from procurement through processing to international shipment.

Our factory in Ullapara, Sirajgonj sits at the heart of one of Bangladesh's most fertile sesame-growing regions. With a port office in Chattogram, we maintain direct control over logistics, documentation, and quality at every stage.

Every batch is SGS-tested. Every shipment is fully documented. Every buyer gets a focused, expert partner.

Head Office — Dhaka

Commercial operations, buyer relations, export management

Port Office — Chattogram

Shipping coordination, C&F liaison, documentation

Factory — Ullapara

Origin

Sourced from the Sirajgonj sesame belt — direct relationships with farms across the district. Every lot travels a short inland route to our Ullapara crushing line, then moves down to Chattogram for onward shipment.

Manufacturing

Five stages,
full traceability

01

Seed Sourcing

Selected from trusted farms across the Sirajgonj sesame belt for premium raw material quality.

02

Cleaning & Drying

Multi-stage mechanical cleaning followed by drying to optimal moisture levels for quality preservation.

03

Oil Crushing

Selected seeds crushed to extract crude sesame oil, preserving natural aroma and nutrients.

04

Quality Testing

SGS-certified laboratory testing for acidity, moisture, color, and purity.

05

Pack & Ship

Packed in flexitank, drums, or PP oven bags. Full export documentation from LC to Bill of Lading.
