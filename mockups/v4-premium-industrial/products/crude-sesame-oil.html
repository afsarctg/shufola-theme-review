§ 01.  ·  Our Core Product  ·  Crude

CRUDE SESAME OIL

Crude sesame oil is the first pressing from our selected seeds at the Ullapara facility. It ships to international refineries and premium food manufacturers. The cake is held separately for second-grade applications — feed, industrial, and select soap-grade markets.

Every batch is tested by SGS Bangladesh for moisture, free fatty acid, acidity, colour, peroxide value, and iodine value. The certificate of analysis ships with each lot. Typical ranges are listed below; exact values per shipment.

Flexitank is our standard container at 22 metric tons. Steel drums at 190 kilograms and IBC totes at 1,000 litres are available for smaller orders. CFR, FOB, and CIF incoterms are all supported. LC-negotiation and full export documentation stay with our Dhaka export desk.

02. Applications Markets Refinery · Food mfg · Specialty

THREE
PRIMARY MARKETS.
ONE UPSTREAM PRESS.

Crude sesame oil from a single-crop operation lets buyers specify for refinement, direct food manufacturing, or a premium crude fraction — from the same upstream source.

01.   Refinery

Neutralise · Bleach · Deodorise

Refineries process crude sesame oil into consumer-grade refined oil through neutralisation, bleaching, and deodorisation. Our crude's low FFA and consistent iodine value reduce refinement loss.

02.   Food Manufacturing

Tahini · Bakery · Confectionery

Food manufacturers (tahini producers, bakeries, confectioners, specialty cuisine) use crude directly for its aroma profile. Our first-press fraction is held separate for this market.

03.   Specialty Consumer

Origin-Specific Premium Grades

Specialty and high-end consumer grades increasingly demand origin-specific crude — our Sirajgonj-district supply answers that demand.
