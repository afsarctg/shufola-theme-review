05. Process Sourcing → Ship 5 stages · 2-3 weeks per lot

FIVE STAGES.
ONE SPECIFICATION.
ONE FLOOR.

Every lot travels the same five stages in the same order. What changes lot to lot is the density of tests, the buyer's packing spec, and the Incoterm.

01
Stage · Yard Intake

Seed Sourcing

Selected from trusted farms across the Sirajgonj sesame belt for premium raw material quality. Every sack arrives coded to a farmer and a planting lot; nothing enters the cleaning floor without a yard log entry.

Lot coded · Traceable · Sirajgonj belt
02
Stage · Cleaning Line

Cleaning & Drying

Multi-stage mechanical cleaning followed by drying to optimal moisture levels for quality preservation. The Bühler fine-fraction line handles sizing; the drying floor takes the remainder.

Moisture ≤ 0.2% · Bühler line
03
Stage · Crush Floor

Oil Crushing

Selected seeds crushed to extract crude sesame oil, preserving natural aroma and nutrients. The first-pressing fraction is what buyers ask for by name; the cake goes to feed and soap applications, tracked separately.

First-press fraction · FFA ≤ 5%
04
Stage · QC Laboratory

Quality Testing

SGS-certified laboratory testing for acidity, moisture, colour, peroxide value, iodine value, and purity. The certificate of analysis travels with the lot; a retained sample stays with us for two years.

SGS Bangladesh · CoA per lot
05
Stage · Export Dock

Pack & Ship

Packed in flexitank at 22 MT, steel drums at 190 kg, IBC totes at 1000 L, or PP oven bags for seed lots. Full export documentation from LC to Bill of Lading handled in-house. Cleared through Chattogram.

Flexitank · Drum · IBC · Docs in-house
