Our core product · Section 01

Crude Sesame Oil

Crushed from carefully selected seeds. Deep golden color, rich natural aroma. Exported to refineries and food manufacturers across 15+ countries.

I.

First pressing. Fully documented.

Crude sesame oil is the first pressing from our selected seeds at the Ullapara facility. We hold the cake separately for second-grade applications; the first-press fraction is what ships to international refineries and premium food manufacturers.

Every batch is tested by SGS Bangladesh for moisture, free fatty acid, acidity, and color before it leaves our warehouse. The certificate of analysis ships with the lot.

We work in full container loads (FCL) with flexitank as standard; drums and IBC totes available for smaller orders. LC-negotiation and complete export documentation handled in-house.
